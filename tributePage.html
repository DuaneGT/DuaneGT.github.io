Pacencia Gayaman Gauken Golocan

A mother of 7, a grandmother of 21, and a great-grandmother of 27

Here's a time line of Pacencia's life:

  • 1937 - Born in San Gabriel, La Union
  • 1958 - Married to Pedro A. Golocan Sr.
  • 1959 - Became a mother at the age of 20 years old.
  • 1989 - Went to the United States due to an immigration petition filed by her eldest daughter.
  • 1990 - Has started working odd jobs to help her eldest daughter in financial situations.
  • 1992 - Repatriated in the Philippines to help out his husband Pedro in farming.
  • 1996 - Julius, her youngest son died from a gunshot wound, and decided to take his daughter, Noemie, in her care.
  • 1998 - Decides to stay in Baguio City to help take care of his 12th grandson, Duane.
  • 2001 - Together with her husband, Pedro, They bought an expansion of their farmland in Isabela.
  • 2006 - Dies at the age of 69 due to heart complications.
